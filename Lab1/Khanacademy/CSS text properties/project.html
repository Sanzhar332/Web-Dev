Lionel Messi wins record seventh men's Ballon d'Or

SkySports
Posted on 30th November of 2021

Lionel Messi has won the men's Ballon d'Or for a record seventh time.Messi, who left Barcelona as a free agent to sign for Paris Saint-Germain in the summer, was presented with the award for best men's footballer in the world on Monday night in Paris after another record-breaking 12 months for club and country.

The World Cup belongs to Lionel Messi

ESPN
Posted on 21 december of 2022

Winning the FIFA World Cup with Argentina has been Messi's number one goal all his career, in 2014 Argentina and Messi lost the World Cup final to Germany.At the 2022 World Cup, they started with the defeat of the Saudi Arabian national team, but then they did not lose more than one match.In the final, Messi met with his Psg teammate Killian Mbappe, this match of Argentina against France will remain in memory for many years to come, after such a passionate match, Messi still raised the world Cup with the Argentina national team as a witness.This trophy not only closed his gestalt but also he won all the tournaments where he participated and Messi was also recognized as the best player of the entire World Cup that was held in Qatar
