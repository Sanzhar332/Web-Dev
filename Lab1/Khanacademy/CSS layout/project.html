You're invited to ceremony Ballon D'or 2022

For the first time, the Ballon D’Or will be given to the best player in a season and not a calendar year.

The Ballon D’Or 2022 awards to honour the best footballers for the 2021-22 season will be held in Paris.For the first time in history, the Ballon D’Or - awarded by France Football - will recognise the best football player in the men’s and women’s categories over the course of a season. It was previously given to the best player in a calendar year.

Karim Benzema is the favourite to win the men’s Ballon D’Or 2022. The French striker enjoyed a stellar season with Real Madrid, helping them win the La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles in 2021-22.

Benzema scored 27 goals in the La Liga and 15 goals in the UCL, including hat-tricks against Chelsea and PSG in the knockouts, and finished as the top-scorer in both competitions.
